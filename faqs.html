Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about blood donation

Who can donate blood?

Anyone aged 18-65, in good health, and meeting donation requirements can donate blood.

How to donate blood?

Visit a certified blood donation center or mobile drive, undergo a health screening, donate, and rest for a short period after donation.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by visiting our website, calling a local blood donation center, or using our mobile app.
